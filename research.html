Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of robotics, control systems, and avionics. I work on system integration, hardware/software-in-the-loop (HIL) testbeds, and safety-critical embedded systems for aerospace. On the academic side, I focus on surgical/medical robotics, sensorized instruments, and compliant human-robot interaction. I am also exploring cooperative manipulation, perception, and intelligent control strategies for next-gen robotic systems.


Currently, I am developing modular SIL/HIL platforms at NASTP for avionics testing and pursuing graduate research in surgical robotics and cooperative manipulation.
